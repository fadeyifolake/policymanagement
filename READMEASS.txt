
# Policy Management System

## Overview

This project implements a policy management system for an insurance company. It consists of separate classes for managing policyholders, products, and payments. The system allows policy managers to perform various tasks such as adding and suspending policyholders, registering new members, managing policy products, processing payments, sending reminders, and applying penalties.

## Assignment Tasks

### 1. Class Creation

- **Policyholder Class**: Manages policyholders, including methods for registration, suspension, and reactivation.
- **Product Class**: Manages policy products, including methods for creation, updating, and suspension/removal.
- **Payment Class**: Manages payments, including methods for processing, sending reminders, and applying penalties.

### 2. Policyholder Management

- **Register Policyholders**: Implement a method to register new policyholders.
- **Suspend/Reactivate Policyholders**: Implement methods to suspend and reactivate policyholders as needed.

### 3. Payment Management

- **Process Payments**: Implement a method to process payments for policyholders.
- **Send Reminders**: Implement a method to send payment reminders to policyholders.
- **Apply Penalties**: Implement a method to apply penalties for late payments.

### 4. Product Management

- **Create Products**: Implement methods to create new policy products.
- **Update Products**: Implement methods to update existing policy products.
- **Suspend/Remove Products**: Implement methods to suspend or remove policy products.

### 5. Policyholder Demonstration

- **Create Demo Policyholders**: Create at least two policyholders who have paid for one of the products.
- **Display Account Details**: Display the account details of the demo policyholders.
